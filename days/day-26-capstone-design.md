# Day 26: Capstone Design

## Objective

Choose one approved capstone scenario track and define a realistic scope.

## Core Topics

- Problem statement
- Target user
- Approved scenario track
- Inputs and outputs
- Minimum viable project
- Risks and assumptions
- Success criteria

## Lesson Content

A good beginner capstone is small, understandable, and demonstrable. The goal is not complexity. The goal is to show Microsoft AI, DevOps, DevSecOps, and AIOps fundamentals through a complete project.

Participants must choose one track:

- Learning Assistant
- Community FAQ Assistant
- Operations Helper

## Hands-On Lab

1. Choose one approved capstone scenario track.
2. Fill in the project submission template as a draft.
3. Create `project/project-plan.md`.
4. Identify which Microsoft Foundry or Azure AI Services capability your project will use or map to.
5. Define what will be included and what will be out of scope.

## Deliverable

- `project/project-plan.md`

## Mentor Notes

Help learners reduce scope. Small and finished is the win.

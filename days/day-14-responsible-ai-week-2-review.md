# Day 14: Responsible AI and Week 2 Review

## Objective

Understand common AI risks and review Python and AI fundamentals.

## Core Topics

- Hallucination
- Bias
- Privacy
- Security
- Human review
- Responsible use

## Lesson Content

AI output can be useful but wrong. Responsible AI means thinking carefully about accuracy, fairness, safety, privacy, and who is affected by the system. Beginners should learn early that AI answers need review.

## Hands-On Lab

1. Ask an AI assistant to explain a technical topic.
2. Identify three claims that should be checked.
3. Write a responsible AI note for your bootcamp README.
4. Summarize Week 2.

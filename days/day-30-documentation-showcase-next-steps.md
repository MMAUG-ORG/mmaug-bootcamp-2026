# Day 30: Documentation, Showcase, and Next Steps

## Objective

Finalize the capstone, present it clearly, and choose the next learning path.

## Core Topics

- README quality
- Prize eligibility criteria
- Responsible AI notes
- Demo preparation
- Reflection
- Feedback
- Portfolio next steps

## Lesson Content

The final day is about communicating the work. A beginner who can explain what they built, what worked, what failed, and what they would improve next has made real progress.

## Hands-On Lab

1. Finalize the capstone README.
2. Add responsible AI and limitations sections.
3. Confirm the project submission template is complete.
4. Check the prize eligibility criteria if submitting for voucher consideration.
5. Prepare a 3-minute demo outline.
6. Write a final reflection.
7. Submit the project link.

## Deliverable

- Final capstone submission
- `project/demo-outline.md`
- `notes/final-reflection.md`

## Mentor Notes

Celebrate completion and point learners toward specific next steps.

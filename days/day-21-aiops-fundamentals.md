# Day 21: AIOps Fundamentals

## Objective

Understand how AI can support operations, monitoring, and incident response.

## Core Topics

- AIOps
- Logs
- Metrics
- Traces
- Alerts
- Incidents
- Anomaly detection
- AI-assisted troubleshooting

## Lesson Content

AIOps applies AI and data-driven techniques to IT operations. At a beginner level, learners should understand that systems produce signals such as logs, metrics, and alerts. AI can help summarize, group, or investigate these signals, but humans still need to verify conclusions and decide what action is safe.

## Hands-On Lab

1. Read a sample error message from one of your scripts or workflows.
2. Write a plain-language incident summary.
3. Draft three prompts that could help investigate the issue.
4. Write what evidence you would verify before acting.
